    About us
    Orbit Freight Logistics Services Agency (OFL ) is a fully independent Arabian logistics company focused on international and global transport solutions for GCC exporters and importers.OFL offers complete international forwarding and logistics solutions using all modes of transport: air, sea, road, rail, single and multimodal. We also offer specifically tailored solutions for transports of live animals, intermediate goods to the shipping and offshore industries, aerospace industry and defence logistics, all over the World.Our partners are all specialists in their respective domestic markets, and through our close relationships we can ensure the best solution for our customers regardless of country or destination. Our handpicked staff has extensive experience and broad competence, and we work actively to safeguard our ability to offer competitive and innovative logistics solutions to surpass our customers’ expectations. In partnership with customers and staff, OFL strives at providing sustainable logistics solutions with a stronger commitment, accountability and reliability. We are easy to deal with, dedicated and passionate about our tasks. As our customer you will therefore have time to focus on your own business.
